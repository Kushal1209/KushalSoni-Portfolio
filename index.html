About Me

Hi, my name is Kushal Soni, and I hail from the vibrant city of Vadodara. Throughout my academic journey, I have been fortunate to receive a quality education that has shaped me into the person I am today.

My schooling took place at Urmi School. After completing my schooling, I pursued my passion for computer science by enrolling in the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at Navrachana University. During my time there, I delved deep into the world of computer programming, software development, and information technology. The rigorous curriculum and hands-on projects allowed me to hone my technical skills and cultivate a problem-solving mindset.

Currently, I am pursuing my Master's in Computer Applications (MCA) from Charusat University. This program has further expanded my knowledge and expertise in areas such as web development, database management, software engineering, software's quality assurance, data analysis, etc.

I am a well-qualified Full Stack Developer familiar with wide range of programming utilities and languages. Knowledgeable of backend and frontend development requirements. Handles any part of process with ease. Collaborative team player with excellent technical abilities offering close to 1.5 years of related experience.
